More Than Just Treasure Voyages

This is the revised version of my final project for Geo370 from 2014. The inital map was not taking visual hierachy into serious consideration, which this version has improved greatly on. This map poster is not intended to capture the precise routes of each voyage. Its purpose is to inform the map readers of the significance of Zheng He's voyages, and to inspire people to explore Chinese history. See more

Zhe He is one of the most famouse voyagers and explorers in history. He is one of the most well-known diplomist officiers in Chinese history. I've learned about his stories and how his seven voyages to many countries have changed the way ancient China see the world. More importantly, my hometown is also Kunming China, same as Zheng He!
