Research the Icy Land

This is a project looking at the categorization of Antarctic research projects. The categorization is based on the goals of the projects through literature review of the Antarctic research projects' summaries. The message that the map conveys is that the main trend of current Antarctic research is still focusing on understanding the region of Antarctica and its ecosystem, based on the fact that the most research stations have the most amount of research projects on researching the Antarctic region.

The other reason for making this map is out of my curiosity for the Antarctic region. Through reviewing the project summaries. I was able to gain information on the projects, the research stations, and the culture of the research stations. I'm hoping to become a research assistant in one of the Antarctic research project post graduation. The process of making this map is a starting point for me to get to know the region.
